  <h2>üí´ About Me üí´</h2>
</div>

<p align="justify">
I specialize in <span style="color:#ff64da;font-weight:bold">AI-driven software development</span>, <span style="color:#ff64da;font-weight:bold">deep learning</span>, and <span style="color:#ff64da;font-weight:bold">medical image processing</span>, with a strong focus on explainable AI (XAI), feature selection, and scalable AI architectures. My expertise extends to bioinformatics, AI-powered IoT systems, and intelligent automation, where I build high-performance AI applications for real-world challenges.
</p>

<p align="justify">
Currently, I am working as an <span style="color:#ff64da;font-weight:bold">AI Software Engineer at DIU AI Research Lab</span>, where I develop AI-powered solutions, manage research-driven projects, and optimize AI systems for scalability and efficiency. Additionally, I serve as a <span style="color:#ff64da;font-weight:bold">Research Assistant at the Centre for Advanced Machine Learning and Applications (CAMLAs)</span>, contributing to cutting-edge research in medical AI, computational biology, and feature selection techniques.
</p>

<p align="justify">
With a deep background in software engineering, I develop scalable AI-driven platforms using FastAPI, PyTorch, and React, optimizing AI inference pipelines, real-time data processing, and cloud-based AI systems. My work integrates computer vision, medical AI, and feature engineering to improve model interpretability and efficiency.
</p>
